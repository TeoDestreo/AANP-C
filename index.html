Why We Focus on Nonprofits

Nonprofits have unique accounting and reporting needs. Restricted grants, donor reporting, functional expenses, board oversight, and public accountability all require financial systems that are more disciplined than basic bookkeeping.

Our work is designed to help nonprofit leaders spend less time guessing about the numbers and more time making decisions with reliable financial information.
